Eloping

Planning to keep things quiet and take off to your honeymoon?

I offer a no fuss, no ceremony eloping service.

Like every marriage, you will still need 2 witnesses.

We can meet at your home, a coffee shop or even the airport for an authentic eloping! Quick and easy.

Please note that elopings still require the couple to get the obligatory paper work from their municipality or province and that I do not offer eloping services on Fridays or Saturdays.
