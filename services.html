Services

Marriage Ceremony

Your love. Your day. Your ceremony.

Marriage is a special union of individual, unique people. Your ceremony deserves to be as unique as you are, and each element of your ceremony should be a reflection of your love. It’s your story; it may be as long or short as you want it to be, involving as many or few elements you love. What you want is what we make happen, together.

Together, we create a place in time for your romance story to be honoured, shared and unified.

Vow Renewal

Honouring a milestone

I create personalized ceremonies honouring a pinnacle of time together or a milestone in your relationship. Together we can recreate the wedding you had, or create the dream ceremony you’ve always wanted.

With your help, we will create a special moment in time to commemorate your love and promises to each other.

Elopement

No ceremony, no problem!

For some, eloping means sharing your special union intimately and quietly between yourselves and your closest friends. Maybe you want the experience to be in your home or at the place you first met. For others, eloping means meeting at the airport quickly before taking off to a honeymoon!

However you choose, we will make your elopement exactly the way you want it.

Please note that two witnesses are still legally required.

Fees

Due to the nature of custom and personalized services for each unique couple, there are no standard fees. My fees are in accordance with the standard of the geographical area.

I aim to create your vision within your budget as best as possible. After an initial discussion about the desired services, I will conduct a review of what is required, and within 48 hours you will be provided with a quote for your ceremony.

If you decide I am the right person to officiate your love, we will then discuss the deposit and due date of the full payment.
